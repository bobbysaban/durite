Countertops: Material Science

UV Resistant

Kitchen and bar countertops are subjected to many internal and external environment factors, such as UV's emitted by natural and artificial light. Durite’s countertops will stay white due to titanium DI-oxide, which is stable under UV.

Anti-Bacterial + Anti-Fungal for food safety

Food safety is the most important concern when it comes to countertops and Durite countertop are impermeable and water/moisture resistant so as bacteria is unable to grow.

Stain-resistant

Durite countertop have a natural finish by a process known as burnishing which is the result of constant polishing where stones are heated to a natural shine. This process also closes surface pores and prevents harmful substances such as Lime/Lemon Juice, salts, and oils to stain or dissolve the countertop.

Incredible Strength

Durite manufactured countertops are made to handle harsh environments at a molecular level. Our countertop surfaces and “Brittle” strength (+/- 10KSI) so that the top can be used as “Cantilever”

Manufacturing Process

3D Single Casting

Durite countertops are created by 3D casting which is a single cast that creates seamless and monolithic creations. This technique has many advantages as its made from the same materials through and through and have no joints that separate under heavy loading.

Fusing

This incredible feature allows us to ship counters and bar tops in pieces and glue them on site to make them one.
