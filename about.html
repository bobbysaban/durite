About Durite:

Durite is one of the leading companies in fabrication, manufacturing and design of surfacing materials for both interior and exterior as well as horizontal (flooring) and vertical (walls) surfaces.

Durite fabricates and manufactures, shower basins, kitchen & bar counter tops, vanity tops, art panels, furniture and fixtures of almost all types that can be Manufactured or Fabricated.

Durite is a patent technology that uses the ability of Chemistry & Polymers for single bonding, DzFusingdz and creating seamless (endless) lengths, widths and vertical surfaces.

All of our Manufacturing is made in the USA. imports are limited to finish slabs from Man-Made Materials, Quartz, Natural or Exotic materials that are requested.
